Replicate Us

A key goal of this project was to identify the best ways to recycle E-waste, and make it easy for others to take advantage of our work.

Specifically, we are releasing the following key pieces of our project under the BSD 3-Clause Open Source license.

We hope people will copy our project and customize it to their community. Feel free to suggest
improvements - you can send us update suggestions through our Facebook page, as well as through
GitHub.

  • Project report
  • Source code on GitHub: this includes articles, links to resources, graphics, etc.
  • Posters: Drop Box, Yard Sign, School Awareness, Recycling Facts 1, Recycling Facts 2, Recycling Facts 3, What to Recycle
