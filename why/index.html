Why EasyRecycle

Currently, recycling phones is hard

Our field research shows that 90% of the adult population has at least one unused mobile phone in their household. Of these, 70% do not recycle because they don’t know how, or it’s too inconvenient to recycle.

For example, to recycle a phone with the City of Austin, you’d have to drive 20 miles each way to 290 and I-35 which is where the only city-run recycling service is available where you can recycle a phone.
